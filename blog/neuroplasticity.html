Neuroplasticity


Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

Better Learning - Neuroplasticity shows that our brain can grow and adapt which helps to learn new things by using different methods and practicing regularly.
Recovery from Injuries - Neuroplasticity shows that it can helps the brain after injury to heal by creating new pathways. As physical and mental exercises can help people to regain abilities they may have lost.
Breaking bad Habits - Neuroplasticity shows that it can help people to break bad habits like smoking and overeating. Relating postive behaviour can re-wire the brain making stick with new healthy habits.
Staying sharp with Age - Neuroplasticity shows that it can help to keep minds sharp by doing activities like solving puzzles, learning new skills or playing games can help to prevent memory loss and keep the brain active.

Growth Mindset


Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

A Growth Mindset is the belief that intelligence and abilities can be developed through effort, learning and determination. Opposite to growth mindset its fixed mindset where people are their abilities as a static.


In this exploration, did anything surprise you. Change for you.

A surprising realization is how much a Growth Mindset can reshape everyday life. I found that simply viewing challenges as chances to learn, rather than as threats, makes a big difference in how we approach and overcome obstacles. It shifts the focus from fear of failure to growth and improvement.


How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey.

Never quit what you started and keep learning. Sometimes it will take time but if you stay persistent and focused and keep grow it will eventually see progress.
